What is Lab-Grown Meat?

Lab-grown meat, also known as clean meat, is a type of meat that is grown in a laboratory using animal cells. This innovative technology allows for the production of meat without the need for animal slaughter, reducing the environmental impact of traditional livestock farming.

Benefits

  • Reduced environmental impact
  • Increased food safety
  • Improved animal welfare
  • Customizable nutrition and taste

How it Works

The process of creating lab-grown meat involves several steps:

  1. Harvesting animal cells
  2. Separating muscle and fat cells
  3. Culturing muscle cells in a favorable artificial environment
  4. Allowing the cells to merge and form muscle tissue
  5. Layering the tissue to form a hamburger shape
